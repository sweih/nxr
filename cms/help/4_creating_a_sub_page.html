Creating a sub page

A typical site structure contains a homepage and sub pages that form a tree-like hierachy. A homepage could be viewed as the introduction to the site, whereas sub pages provide more detailed information on the topics introduced on the homepage. Sub pages and folders are essential in organising the content of a large site.

Note: After you have created your homepage, you can continue and create sub pages using the same steps that were described in section 3. The only difference is that you click the Add Page command on a lower level in the Browse Sitemap menu tree than when creating the homepage.

  1. Choose Website in the Content menu.

    Browse Sitemap is displayed.

  2. Click on the (+) image next to the object where you want to add a new sub page.
  3. See the step-by-step instructions in section Creating a homepage to proceed.

Note: When creating your homepage or sub pages, remember the level you are working on while proceeding.

Creating a homepage

Home page is the main page of a web site created by N/X. The home page serves as an index or table of contents to other documents stored at the site. A home page may include elements such as an introduction of a company and links to other parts of the site.

Creating a page

Adding a new page

  1. Choose Content from the menu on top of your N/X working area. Choose Website from the dropdown menu.

    2. Browse Sitemap menu is displayed.

  2. Choose Add Page.

    3. Add Page menu is displayed.

  3. Select a template by clicking one of the alternatives on the right.
  4. Enter the name of the page in the blank namespace.
  5. Choose the position of your page on the sitemap from the dropdown menu.
  6. Tick the box beside Create content for this page if you wish to create content for your page.
  7. Click Create. An editing space is displayed.

Creating menu information

  1. Choose Menu from the menu bar on top of the page.
  2. Add the name of your page in the Name on Menu space. You can use the same name you did previously or a new one.
  3. You can add a menu helptext in the Helptext for Menu space.
  4. Enter the direct URL of the page in the URL space.
  5. Click Save.

Launching the page

  1. Press Edit content on top of the page. An editing space is displayed.
  2. Click Launch Page Icon. Your page will be visible on the website after a few minutes' delay.
